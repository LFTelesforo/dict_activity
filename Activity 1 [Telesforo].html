Adobo Recipe

nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom nom

Welcome to a delightful journey of flavors with our special Adobo recipe. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a cooking enthusiast, this recipe is sure to satisfy your taste buds!

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 6 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 4 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • secret recipe, rawr
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn

Instructions

  1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic in a large bowl.
    Mix well. Marinate the chicken for at least 1 hour. Note: the longer the time, the better
  2. Heat a cooking pot. Pour cooking oil.
  3. When the oil is hot enough, pan-fry the marinated chicken for 2 minutes per side.
  4. Pour-in the remaining marinade, including garlic. Add water. Bring to a boil
  5. Add dried bay leaves and whole peppercorn. Simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken gets tender
  6. Add vinegar. Stir and cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Put-in the sugar, and salt. Stir and turn the heat off. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!

Additional Information

  • Serving Suggestions: Adobo pairs well with steamed rice or your favorite side dish.
  • Tips: For a richer flavor, marinate the chicken overnight. Adjust soy sauce and vinegar according to your taste.
  • Variations: Try adding coconut milk for a creamy twist or experiment with different meats like pork or beef.
